Abraham become the father of Isaac , Isaac the father of Jacob , Jacob the father of Judah and his brother .        Judah become the father of Perez and Zerah , whose mother be Tamar .    Perez become the father of Hezron , Hezron the father of Ram ,       Ram the father of Amminadab . Amminadab become the father of Nahshon , Nahshon the father of Salmon ,       Salmon the father of Boaz , whose mother be Rahab . Boaz become the father of Obed , whose mother be Ruth . Obed become the father of Jesse ,       Jesse the father of David the king . David become the father of Solomon , whose mother have be the wife of Uriah .        Solomon become the father of Rehoboam , Rehoboam the father of Abijah , Abijah the father of Asaph .      Asaph become the father of Jehoshaphat , Jehoshaphat the father of Joram , Joram the father of Uzziah .      Uzziah become the father of Jotham , Jotham the father of Ahaz , Ahaz the father of Hezekiah .      Hezekiah become the father of Manasseh , Manasseh the father of Amos ,    Amos the father of Josiah .      Josiah become the father of Jechoniah and his brother at the time of the babylonian exile .      after the babylonian exile , Jechoniah become the father of Shealtiel , Shealtiel the father of Zerubbabel ,      Zerubbabel the father of Abiud . Abiud become the father of Eliakim , Eliakim the father of Azor ,      Azor the father of Zadok . Zadok become the father of Achim , Achim the father of Eliud ,      Eliud the father of Eleazar . Eleazar become the father of Matthan , Matthan the father of Jacob ,      Jacob the father of Joseph , the husband of Mary . of she be bear Jesus who be call the Messiah .     thus the total number of generation from Abraham to David be fourteen generation ; from David to the babylonian exile , fourteen generation ; from the babylonian exile to the Messiah , fourteen generation .   the Birth of Jesus .        now this be how the birth of Jesus Christ come about . when his mother Mary be betroth to Joseph ,    but before they live together , she be find with child through the holy Spirit .      Joseph her husband , since he be a righteous man ,    yet unwilling to expose she to shame , decide to divorce she quietly .       such be his intention when , behold , the angel of the Lord    appear to he in a dream and say , Joseph , son of David , do not be afraid to take Mary your wife into your home . for it be through the holy Spirit that this child have be conceive in she .      she will bear a son and you be to name he Jesus ,    because he will save his people from their sin .      all this take place to fulfill what the Lord have say through the prophet :       behold , the virgin shall be with child and bear a son ,   and they shall name he Emmanuel , which mean God be with we .      when Joseph awake , he do as the angel of the Lord have command he and take his wife into his home .      he have no relation with she until she bear a son ,    and he name he Jesus .
