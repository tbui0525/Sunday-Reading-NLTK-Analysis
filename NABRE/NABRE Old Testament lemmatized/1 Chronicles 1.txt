Adam , Seth , Enosh ,        Kenan , Mahalalel , Jared ,        Enoch , Methuselah , Lamech ,        Noah , Shem , Ham , and Japheth .        the son of Japheth be Gomer , Magog , Madai , Javan , Tubal , Meshech , and Tiras .        the son of Gomer be Ashkenaz , Riphath , and Togarmah .      the son of Javan be Elishah , Tarshish , the Kittim , and the Rodanim .     the son of Ham be Cush , Mizraim , Put , and Canaan .      the son of Cush be Seba , Havilah , Sabta , Raama , and Sabteca . the son of Raama be Sheba and Dedan .      Cush become the father of Nimrod , who be the first to be a warrior on the earth .         Mizraim become the father of the Ludim , Anamim , Lehabim , Naphtuhim ,      Pathrusim , Casluhim , and Caphtorim , from whom the Philistines spring .      Canaan become the father of Sidon , his firstborn , and Heth ,      and the Jebusites , the Amorites , the Girgashites ,      the Hivites , the Arkites , the Sinites ,      the Arvadites , the Zemarites , and the Hamathites .      the son of Shem be Elam , Asshur , Arpachshad , Lud , and Aram . the son of Aram be Uz , Hul , Gether , and Mash .      Arpachshad become the father of Shelah , and Shelah become the father of Eber .      two son be bear to Eber ; the first be name Peleg ( for in his time the world be divide ) ,    and his brother be name Joktan .      Joktan become the father of Almodad , Sheleph , Hazarmaveth , Jerah ,      Hadoram , Uzal , Diklah ,      Ebal , Abimael , Sheba ,      Ophir , Havilah , and Jobab ; all these be the son of Joktan .      Shem , Arpachshad , Shelah ,      Eber , Peleg , Reu ,      Serug , Nahor , Terah ,      Abram , that is , Abraham .     from Abraham to Jacob .     the son of Abraham be Isaac and Ishmael .        these be their generation : Nebaioth , the firstborn of Ishmael , then Kedar , Adbeel , Mibsam ,      Mishma , Dumah , Massa , Hadad , Tema ,      Jetur , Naphish , and Kedemah . these be the son of Ishmael .      the son of Keturah , Abrahams concubine : she bear Zimran , Jokshan , Medan , Midian , Ishbak , and Shuah . the son of Jokshan be Sheba and Dedan .      the son of Midian be Ephah , Epher , Hanoch , Abida , and Eldaah . all these be the son of Keturah .      Abraham begot Isaac . the son of Isaac be Esau and Israel .      the descendant of Esau be Eliphaz , Reuel , Jeush , Jalam , and Korah .      the descendant of Eliphaz be Teman , Omar , Zephi , Gatam , Kenaz , Timna , and Amalek .      the descendant of Reuel be Nahath , Zerah , Shammah , and Mizzah .      the son of Seir    be Lotan , Shobal , Zibeon , Anah , Dishon , Ezer , and Dishan .      the son of Lotan be Hori and Homam ; Timna be the sister of Lotan .      the son of Shobal be Alian , Manahath , ebal , Shephi , and Onam . the son of Zibeon be Aiah and Anah .      the son of Anah : Dishon . the son of Dishon be Hemdan , Eshban , Ithran , and Cheran .      the son of Ezer be Bilhan , Zaavan , and Jaakan . the son of Dishan be Uz and Aran .       the king who reign in the land of Edom before the Israelites have king be the follow : Bela , son of Beor , the name of whose city be Dinhabah .      when Bela die , Jobab , son of Zerah , from Bozrah , succeed he as king .        when Jobab die , Husham , from the land of the Temanites , succeed he as king .        Husham die and Hadad , son of Bedad , succeed he as king . he overthrow the Midianites on the Moabite plateau , and the name of his city be Avith .      Hadad die and Samlah of Masrekah succeed he as king .      Samlah die and Shaul from Rehoboth on the Euphrates succeed he as king .      when Shaul die , Baalhanan , son of Achbor , succeed he as king .      Baalhanan die and Hadad succeed he as king . the name of his city be Pai , and his wife name be Mehetabel . she be the daughter of Matred , who be the daughter of Mezahab .       after Hadad die , there be chief in Edom : the chief of Timna , Aliah , Jetheth ,      Oholibamah , Elah , Pinon ,      Kenaz , Teman , Mibzar ,      Magdiel , and Iram be the chief of Edom .
