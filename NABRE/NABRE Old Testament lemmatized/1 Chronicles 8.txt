Benjamin become the father of Bela , his firstborn , Ashbel , the second son , Aharah , the third ,        Nohah , the fourth , and Rapha , the fifth .      the son of Bela be addar and Gera , the father of Ehud .      the son of Ehud be Abishua , Naaman , Ahoah ,        Gera , Shephuphan , and Huram .      these be the son of Ehud , family head over those who dwelt in Geba and be deport to Manahath .      also Naaman , Ahijah , and Gera . the last , who lead they into exile , become the father of Uzza and Ahihud .      Shaharaim become a father on the Moabite plateau after he have put away his wife Hushim and Baara .      by his wife Hodesh he begot Jobab , Zibia , Mesha , Malcam ,      Jeuz , Sachia , and Mirmah . these be his son , family head .      by Hushim he begot Abitub and Elpaal .      the son of Elpaal be Eber , Misham , Shemed ( who build Ono and Lod with its nearby town ) ,        and Beriah , and Shema . they be family head of those who dwelt in Aijalon , and they put the inhabitant of Gath to flight .      their relative be Elpaal , Shashak , and Jeremoth .      Zebadiah , Arad , Eder ,      Michael , Ishpah , and Joha be the son of Beriah .      Zebadiah , Meshullam , Hizki , Heber ,      Ishmerai , Izliah , and Jobab be the son of Elpaal .      Jakim , Zichri , Zabdi ,      Elienai , Zillethai , Eliel ,      Adaiah , Beraiah , and Shimrath be the son of Shimei .      Ishpan , Eber , Eliel ,      Abdon , Zichri , Hanan ,      Hananiah , Elam , Anthothijah ,      Iphdeiah , and Penuel be the son of Shashak .      Shamsherai , Shehariah , Athaliah ,      Jaareshiah , Elijah , and Zichri be the son of Jeroham .      these be family head in their generation , chief who dwelt in Jerusalem .      in Gibeon dwelt Jeiel , the founder of Gibeon , whose wife name be Maacah ;      also his firstborn son , Abdon , and Zur , Kish , Baal , Ner , Nadab ,      Gedor , Ahio , Zecher , and Mikloth .      Mikloth become the father of Shimeah . these , too , dwelt with their relative in Jerusalem , opposite their fellow tribesman .       Ner become the father of Kish , and Kish become the father of Saul . Saul become the father of Jonathan , Malchishua , Abinadab , and Eshbaal .        the son of Jonathan be Meribbaal , and Meribbaal become the father of Micah .        the son of Micah be Pithon , Melech , Tarea , and Ahaz .      Ahaz become the father of Jehoaddah , and Jehoaddah become the father of Alemeth , Azmaveth , and Zimri . Zimri become the father of Moza .        Moza become the father of Binea , whose son be Raphah , whose son be Eleasah , whose son be Azel .      Azel have six son , whose name be Azrikam , his firstborn , Ishmael , Sheariah , Azariah , Obadiah , and Hanan ; all these be the son of Azel .        the son of Eshek , his brother , be Ulam , his firstborn , Jeush , the second son , and Eliphelet , the third .      the son of Ulam be warrior , skilled with the bow , and they have many son and grandson : one hundred and fifty . all these be the son of Benjamin .
