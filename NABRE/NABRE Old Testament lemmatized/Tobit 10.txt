Tobiahs Return Journey to Nineveh and the Healing of Tobit   Anxiety of the Parents .     meanwhile , day by day , Tobit be keep track of the time Tobiah would need to go and to return . when the number of day be reach and his son do not appear ,      he say , could it be that he have be detain there ? or perhaps Gabael have die , and there be no one to give he the money ?      and he begin to grieve .      his wife Anna say , my son have perish and be no long among the living ! and she begin to weep aloud and to wail over her son :      ala , child , light of my eye , that I have let you make this journey !      but Tobit keep tell she : be still , do not worry , my sister ; he be safe ! probably they have to take care of some unexpected business there . the man who be travel with he be trustworthy and one of our kindred . so do not grieve over he , my sister . he will be here soon .      but she retort , you be still , and do not try to deceive I ! my son have perish ! she would rush out and keep watch every day at the road her son have take . she eat nothing . after the sun have set , she would go back home to wail and cry the whole night through , get no sleep at all .   departure from Ecbatana .   now when the fourteen day of the wedding celebration , which Raguel have swear to hold for his daughter , have come to an end , Tobiah go to he and say : send I off , now , since I know that my father and mother do not believe they will ever see I again . so I beg you , father , let I depart and go back to my own father . I have already tell you how I leave he .      Raguel say to Tobiah : stay , son , stay with I . I be send messenger to your father Tobit , and they will give he news of you .      but Tobiah insist , no , I beg you to send I back to my father .     Raguel then promptly hand over to Tobiah his wife Sarah , together with half of all his property : male and female slave , oxen and sheep , donkey and camel , clothing , money , and household good .      he see they safely off . embrace Tobiah , he say to he : Farewell , son . have a safe journey . may the Lord of heaven grant prosperity to you and to your wife Sarah . and may I see child of your before I die !      then he say to his daughter Sarah , my daughter , honor your father - in - law and your mother - in - law , because from now on they be as much your parent as the one who bring you into the world . go in peace , daughter ; let I hear a good report about you as long as I live . finally he say good - bye to they and let they go . Edna also say to Tobiah : my child and beloved kinsman , may the Lord bring you back safely , and may I live long enough to see child of you and of my daughter Sarah before I die . before the Lord , I entrust my daughter to your care . never cause her grief all the day of your life . go in peace , son . from now on I be your mother , and Sarah be your sister . together may we all prosper throughout the day of our life . she kiss they both and see they safely off .     Tobiah leave Raguel , full of happiness and joy , and he bless the Lord of heaven and earth , the king of all , for make his journey so successful . finally he bless Raguel and his wife Edna , and add , I have be command by the Lord to honor you all the day of your life !
