b '   David in Jerusalem .      Hiram , king of Tyre , send envoy to David along with cedar wood , and mason and carpenter to build he a house .         David now know    that the   Lord   have truly establish he as king over Israel , for his kingdom be greatly exalted for the sake of his people Israel .      David take other wife in Jerusalem and become the father of more son and daughter .      these be the name of those who be bear to he in Jerusalem : Shammua , Shobab , Nathan , Solomon ,      Ibhar , Elishua , Elpelet ,      Nogah , Nepheg , Japhia ,      Elishama , Beeliada , and Eliphelet .     the Philistine Wars .     when the Philistines have hear that David be anoint king over all Israel , they march out in force look for he . but when David hear of this , he go out against they .      meanwhile the Philistines have come and raid the valley of Rephaim .        David inquire of God , shall I attack the Philistines , and will you deliver they into my power ? the   Lord   answer he , Attack , for I have deliver they into your power .      so they attack , at Baal - perazim , and David defeat they there . then David say , by my hand God have break through my enemy just as water break through a dam . therefore that place be call Baal - perazim .        the Philistines abandon their god there , and David order they to be burn .      once again the Philistines raid the valley ,      and again David inquire of God . but God answer he : do not try to pursue they , but go around they and come against they near the balsam tree .      when you hear the sound of marching in the top of the balsam tree , then go forth to battle , for God have already go before you to strike the army of the Philistines .      David do as God command he , and they route the philistine army from Gibeon to Gezer .     thus Davids fame be spread abroad through every land , and the   Lord   put the fear of he on all the nation .
