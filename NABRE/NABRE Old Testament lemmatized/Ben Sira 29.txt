b '   Loans , Alms and Surety    the merciful lend to their neighbor ,   by hold out a help hand , they keep the commandment .    lend to your neighbor in his time of need ,   and pay back your neighbor in time .    keep your promise and be honest with he ,   and at all time you will find what you need .   many borrower ask for a loan   and cause trouble for those who help they .   till he get a loan , he kiss the lender hand   and speak softly of his creditor money , but at time of payment , delay ,   make excuse , and find fault with the timing .   if he can pay , the lender will recover barely half ,   and will consider that a windfall . if he can not pay , the lender be cheat of his money   and acquire an enemy at no extra charge ; with curse and insult the borrower will repay ,   and instead of honor will repay with abuse .   many refuse to lend , not out of meanness ,   but from fear of be cheat needlessly .     but with those in humble circumstance be patient ;   do not keep they wait for your alm .   because of the commandment , help the poor ,   and in their need , do not send they away empty - handed .    lose your money for relative or friend ;   do not hide it under a stone to rot .   Dispose of your treasure accord to the commandment of the most high ,   and that will profit you more than the gold .     store up almsgive in your treasury ,   and it will save you from every evil .   well than a mighty shield and a sturdy spear   it will fight for you against the enemy .      a good person will be surety for a neighbor ,   but whoever have lose a sense of shame will fail he .    do not forget the kindness of your backer ,   for he have give his very life for you .   a sinner will turn the favor of a pledge into misfortune ,    and the ungrateful will abandon his rescuer .   go surety have ruin many who be prosperous   and toss they about like wave of the sea ;   it have exile the prominent   and send they wander through foreign land .   the sinner will come to grief through surety ,   and whoever undertake too much will fall into lawsuit .   help your neighbor accord to your mean ,   but take care lest you fall yourself .    Frugality and its Rewards    Lifes prime need be water , bread , and clothing ,   and also a house for decent privacy .    well be the life of the poor under the shadow of their own roof   than sumptuous banquet among stranger .    whether little or much , be content with what you have :   then you will hear no reproach as a parasite .   it be a miserable life to go from house to house ,   for where you be a guest you dare not open your mouth .   you will entertain and provide drink without be thank ;   besides , you will hear these bitter word :   come here , you parasite , set the table ,   let I eat the food you have there !   go away , you parasite , for one more worthy ;   for my relative visit I need the room !   painful thing to a sensitive person   be rebuke as a parasite and insult from creditor .
