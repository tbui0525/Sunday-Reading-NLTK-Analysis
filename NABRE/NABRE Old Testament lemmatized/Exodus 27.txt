Court of the Tabernacle .      you shall also make a court for the tabernacle . on the south side the court shall have hanging , of fine linen twine , a hundred cubit long ,      with twenty column and twenty pedestal of bronze ; the hook and band on the column shall be of silver .      on the north side there shall be similar hanging , a hundred cubit long , with twenty column and twenty pedestal of bronze ; the hook and band on the column shall be of silver .      on the west side , across the width of the court , there shall be hanging , fifty cubit long , with ten column and ten pedestal .      the width of the court on the east side shall be fifty cubit .      on one side there shall be hanging to the extent of fifteen cubit , with three column and three pedestal ;      on the other side there shall be hanging to the extent of fifteen cubit , with three column and three pedestal .     at the gate of the court there shall be a variegated curtain , twenty cubit long , weave of violet , purple , and scarlet yarn and of fine linen twine . it shall have four column and four pedestal .     all the column around the court shall have band and hook of silver , and pedestal of bronze .      the court be to be one hundred cubit long , fifty cubit wide , and five cubit high . fine linen twine must be use , and the pedestal must be of bronze .      all the fitting of the tabernacle , whatever be their use , as well as all its tent peg and all the tent peg of the court , must be of bronze .    Oil for the Lamps .     you shall command the Israelites to bring you clear oil of crush olive , to be use for the light , so that you may keep lamp burn always .        from evening to morning Aaron and his son shall maintain they before the   Lord   in the tent of meeting , outside the veil which hang in front of the covenant . this shall be a perpetual statute for the Israelites throughout their generation .
