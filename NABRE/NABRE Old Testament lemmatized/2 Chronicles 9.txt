Solomon explain to she everything she ask about , and there be nothing so obscure that Solomon could not explain it to she .       when the queen of Sheba witness Solomons great wisdom , the house he have build ,      the food at his table , the seating of his minister , the attendance and dress of his waiter , his cupbearer and their dress , and the burn offering he sacrifice in the house of the   Lord , it take her breath away .      the report I hear in my country about your deed and your wisdom be true , she tell the king .      I do not believe the report until I come and see with my own eye that not even the half of your great wisdom have be tell I . you have surpass the report I hear .      happy your servant , happy these minister of your , who stand before you always and listen to your wisdom .      bless be the   Lord , your God , who be pleased to set you on his throne as king for the   Lord , your God . in the love your God have for Israel , to establish they forever , he have make you king over they to carry out judgment and justice .      then she give the king one hundred and twenty gold talent , a very large quantity of spice , and precious stone . never again do anyone bring such an abundance of spice as the queen of Sheba give to King Solomon .     the servant of Huram and of Solomon who bring gold from Ophir also bring cabinet wood and precious stone .      with the cabinet wood the king make stair for the house of the   Lord   and the house of the king , and harp and lyre for the chanter . the like of these have not be see before in the land of Judah .      King Solomon give the queen of Sheba everything she desire and ask for , more than she have bring to the king . then she return with her servant to her own country .       the gold that come to Solomon in one year weigh six hundred and sixty - six gold talent ,      in addition to what come from the toll on traveler and what the merchant bring . all the king of Arabia also , and the governor of the country , bring gold and silver to Solomon .     King Solomon make two hundred large shield of beat gold ( six hundred shekel of gold go into each shield )      and three hundred buckler of beat gold ( three hundred shekel of gold go into each buckler ) ; and the king put they in the house of the Forest of Lebanon .     the king make a large ivory throne , and overlay it with fine gold .      the throne have six step ; a footstool of gold be fasten to the throne , and there be an arm on each side of the seat , with two lion stand next to the arm ,      and twelve other lion stand there on the step , two to a step . nothing like this be make in any other kingdom .      all King Solomons drinking vessel be gold , and all the utensil in the house of the Forest of Lebanon be pure gold . there be no silver , for in Solomons time silver be reckon as nothing .      for the king have ship that go to Tarshish with the servant of Huram . once every three year the fleet of Tarshish ship would come with a cargo of gold , silver , ivory , ape , and monkey .
