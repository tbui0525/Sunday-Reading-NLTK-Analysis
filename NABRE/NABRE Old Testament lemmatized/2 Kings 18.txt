b '   Reign of Hezekiah .     in the third year of Hoshea , son of Elah , king of Israel , Hezekiah , son of Ahaz , king of Judah , become king .      he be twenty - five year old when he become king , and he reign twenty - nine year in Jerusalem . his mother name be Abi , daughter of Zechariah .     he do what be right in the   Lord s sight , just as David his father have do .      it be he who remove the high place , shatter the pillar , cut down the asherah ,    and smash the bronze serpent Moses have make , because up to that time the Israelites be burn incense to it . ( it be call Nehushtan . )        he put his trust in the   Lord , the God of Israel ; and neither before nor after he be there anyone like he among all the king of Judah .      Hezekiah hold fast to the   Lord   and never turn away from follow he , but observe the commandment the   Lord   have give Moses .      the   Lord   be with he , and he succeed in all he set out to do . he rebel against the king of Assyria and do not serve he .      it be he who strike the Philistines as far as Gaza , and all its territory from guard post to garrisoned town .      in the fourth year of King Hezekiah , which be the seventh year of Hoshea , son of Elah , king of Israel , Shalmaneser , king of Assyria , attack Samaria and lay siege to it ,       and after three year they capture it . in the sixth year of Hezekiah , the ninth year of Hoshea , king of Israel , Samaria be take .      the king of Assyria then deport the Israelites to Assyria and lead they off to Halah , and the Habor , a river of Gozan , and the city of the Medes .      this happen because they do not obey the   Lord , their God , but violate his covenant ; they do not obey nor do all that Moses , the servant of the   Lord , command .     Sennacherib and Hezekiah .      in the fourteenth year of King Hezekiah , Sennacherib , king of Assyria ,    attack all the fortify city of Judah and capture they .        Hezekiah , king of Judah , send this message to the king of Assyria at Lachish : I have do wrong . leave I , and whatever you impose on I I will bear . the king of Assyria exact three hundred talent of silver and thirty talent of gold from Hezekiah , king of Judah .      Hezekiah give he all the fund there be in the house of the   Lord   and in the treasury of the king house .      at the same time , Hezekiah remove the nave door and the upright of the house of the   Lord , which the king of Judah have order to be overlaid with gold , and give they to the king of Assyria .      the king of Assyria send the general , the lord chamberlain , and the commander    from Lachish with a great army to King Hezekiah at Jerusalem . they go up and come to Jerusalem , to the conduit of the upper pool on the highway of the fuller field , where they take their stand .      they call for the king , but Eliakim , son of Hilkiah , the master of the palace , come out , along with Shebnah the scribe and the chancellor Joah , son of Asaph .      the commander say to they , tell Hezekiah , thus say the great king , the king of Assyria : on what do you base this trust of yours ?      do you think mere word substitute for strategy and might in war ? in whom , then , do you place your trust , that you rebel against I ?      do you trust in Egypt , that broken reed of a staff , which pierce the hand of anyone who lean on it ? that be what Pharaoh , king of Egypt , be to all who trust in he .        or do you people say to I , it be in the   Lord   our God we trust ! ? be it not he whose high place and altar Hezekiah have remove , command Judah and Jerusalem , worship before this altar in Jerusalem ?     now , make a wager with my lord , the king of Assyria : I will give you two thousand horse if you be able to put rider on they .      how then can you turn back even a captain , one of the least servant of my lord , trust , as you do , in Egypt for chariot and horse ?      do I come up to destroy this place without the   Lord ? the   Lord   himself say to I : go up and destroy that land !     then Eliakim , son of Hilkiah , and Shebnah and Joah say to the commander : please speak to your servant in Aramaic ; we understand it . do not speak to we in the language of Judah within earshot of the people who be on the wall .      but the commander reply : be it to your lord and to you that my lord send I to speak these word ? be it not rather to those sit on the wall , who , with you , will have to eat their own excrement and drink their urine ?      then the commander step forward and cry out in a loud voice in the language of Judah , listen to the word of the great king , the king of Assyria .      thus say the king : do not let Hezekiah deceive you , for he can not rescue you from my hand .      and do not let Hezekiah induce you to trust in the   Lord , say , the   Lord   will surely rescue we , and this city will not be hand over to the king of Assyria .      do not listen to Hezekiah , for thus say the king of Assyria : make peace with I , and surrender to I ! eat , each of you , from your vine , each from your own fig tree . drink water , each from your own well ,      until I arrive and take you to a land like your own , a land of grain and wine , a land of bread and vineyard , a land of rich olive and honey . live , and do not die ! and do not listen to Hezekiah when he would incite you by say , the   Lord   will rescue we .      have any of the god of the nation ever rescue his land from the power of the king of Assyria ?      where be the god of Hamath and Arpad ? where be the god of Sepharvaim , Hena , and Ivvah ? do they indeed rescue Samaria from my power ?        which of the god for all these land ever rescue his land from my power ? will the   Lord   then rescue Jerusalem from my power ?      but the people remain silent and do not answer at all , for the king command be , do not answer he .     then the master of the palace , Eliakim , son of Hilkiah , Shebnah the scribe , and the chancellor Joah , son of Asaph , come to Hezekiah with their garment tear , and report to he the word of the commander .
