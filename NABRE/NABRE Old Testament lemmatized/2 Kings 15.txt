Reign of Shallum of Israel .     Shallum , son of Jabesh , become king in the thirty - ninth year of Uzziah , king of Judah ; he reign one month in Samaria .     Menahem , son of Gadi , come up from Tirzah to Samaria , and strike down Shallum , son of Jabesh , in Samaria . he kill he and reign in his place .     as for the rest of the act of Shallum , with the conspiracy he carry out , these be record in the book of the chronicle of the king of Israel .      at that time , Menahem attack Tappuah , all its inhabitant , and its whole district as far as Tirzah , because they do not let he in . he attack they ; he even rip open all their pregnant woman . Pul ,    king of Assyria , come against the land . but Menahem give Pul a thousand talent of silver to have his help in hold onto his kingdom . Menahem rest with his ancestor , and his son Pekahiah succeed he as king .
