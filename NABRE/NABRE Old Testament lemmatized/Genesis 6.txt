Preparation for the Flood .     make yourself an ark of gopherwood ,    equip the ark with various compartment , and cover it inside and out with pitch .      this be how you shall build it : the length of the ark will be three hundred cubit , its width fifty cubit , and its height thirty cubit .        make an opening for daylight    and finish the ark a cubit above it . put the ark entrance on its side ; you will make it with bottom , second and third deck .      I , on my part , be about to bring the flood water on the earth , to destroy all creature under the sky in which there be the breath of life ; everything on earth shall perish .        I will establish my covenant with you . you shall go into the ark , you and your son , your wife and your son wife with you .        of all live creature you shall bring two of every kind into the ark , one male and one female ,    to keep they alive along with you .      of every kind of bird , of every kind of animal , and of every kind of thing that crawl on the ground , two of each will come to you , that you may keep they alive .      moreover , you be to provide yourself with all the food that be to be eat , and store it away , that it may serve as provision for you and for they .      Noah comply ; he do just as God have command he .              '
