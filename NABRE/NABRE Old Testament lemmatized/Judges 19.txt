b '   the Levite from Ephraim .     in those day , when there be no king in Israel ,     there be a Levite reside in remote part of the mountain region of Ephraim    who have take for himself a concubine from Bethlehem of Judah .      but his concubine spurn he and leave he for her father house in Bethlehem of Judah , where she stay for some four month .      her husband then set out with his servant and a pair of donkey , and go after she to soothe she and bring she back . he arrive at her father house , and when the young womans father see he , he come out joyfully to meet he .      his father - in - law , the young womans father , urge he to stay , and so he spend three day eat and drink and pass the night there .      on the fourth day they rise early in the morning and he prepare to go . but the young womans father say to his son - in - law , fortify yourself with a little food ; you can go later on .      so they stay and the two man eat and drink together . then the young womans father say to the husband , why not decide to spend the night here and enjoy yourself ?      the man make a move to go , but when his father - in - law press he he go back and spend the night there .     on the fifth morning he rise early to depart , but the young womans father say , fortify yourself ! he coax he , and he tarry until the afternoon , and the two of they eat .      then when the husband be ready to go with his concubine and servant , the young womans father say to he , see , the day be wear on toward evening . stay for the night . see , the day be come to an end . spend the night here and enjoy yourself . early tomorrow you can start your journey home .      the man , however , refuse to stay another night ; he and his concubine set out with a pair of saddle donkey , and travel until they come opposite Jebus , which be Jerusalem .      since they be near Jebus with the day far go , the servant say to his master , come , let we turn off to this city of the Jebusites and spend the night in it .      but his master say to he , we will not turn off to a foreigner city ,    where there be no Israelites . we will go on to Gibeah .      come , he say to his servant , let we make for some other place and spend the night in either Gibeah or Ramah .        so they continue on their way until the sun set on they when they be opposite Gibeah of Benjamin .      there they turn off to enter Gibeah for the night .    the man go in and sit down in the town square , but no one take they inside to spend the night .      in the evening , however , an old man come from his work in the field ; he be from the mountain region of Ephraim , though he be live in Gibeah where the local people be Benjaminites .       when he notice the traveler in the town square , the old man ask , where be you go , and where have you come from ?      he say to he , we be travel from Bethlehem of Judah far up into the mountain region of Ephraim , where I be from . I have be to Bethlehem of Judah , and now I be go home ; but no one have take I into his house .      we have straw and fodder for our donkey , and bread and wine for myself and for your maidservant and the young man who be with your servant ; there be nothing else we need .      rest assure , the old man say to he , I will provide for all your need , but do not spend the night in the public square .      so he lead they to his house and mixed fodder for the donkey . then they wash their foot , and eat and drink .     the outrage at Gibeah .       while they be enjoy themselves , the man of the city , a bunch of scoundrel , surround the house and beat on the door . they say to the old man who be the owner of the house , bring out the man who have come into your house , so that we may get intimate with he .      the man who be the owner of the house go out to they and say , no , my brother ; do not be so wicked . this man have come into my house ; do not commit this terrible crime .      instead , let I bring out my virgin daughter and this mans concubine . humiliate they , or do whatever you want ; but against he do not commit such a terrible crime .      but the man would not listen to he . so the man seize his concubine and thrust she outside to they . they rape she and abuse she all night until morning , and let she go as the sun be come up .      at the approach of morning the woman come and collapse at the entrance of the house in which her husband be , and lie there until morning .      when her husband rise in the morning and open the door of the house to start out again on his journey , there be the woman , his concubine , collapse at the entrance of the house with her hand on the threshold .      come , let we go , he say to she , but there be no answer . so the man place she on a donkey and start out again for home .      on reach home , he get a knife and take hold of the body of his concubine . he cut she up limb by limb into twelve piece and send they throughout the territory of Israel .        he instruct the man whom he send , thus you shall say to all the man of Israel : have such a thing ever happen from the day the Israelites come up from the land of Egypt to this day ?     take note of it ;
