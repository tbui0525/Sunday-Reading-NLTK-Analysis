b '   improper Mourning Rites .     you be child of the   Lord , your God . you shall not gash yourself nor shave the hair above your forehead for the dead .        for you be a people holy to the   Lord , your God ; the   Lord , your God , have choose you from all the people on the face of the earth to be a people specially his own .     clean and unclean animal .     you shall not eat any abominable thing .         these be the animal you may eat : the ox , the sheep , the goat ,      the deer , the gazelle , the roebuck , the wild goat , the ibex , the antelope , and the mountain sheep .      any among the animal that have divide hoof , with the foot cloven in two , and that chew the cud you may eat .      but you shall not eat any of the following that chew the cud or have cloven hoof : the camel , the hare , and the rock badger , which indeed chew the cud , but do not have divide hoof ; they be unclean for you .      and the pig , which indeed have divide hoof , with cloven foot , but do not chew the cud , be unclean for you . their flesh you shall not eat , and their dead body you shall not touch .     these you may eat , of all that live in the water : whatever have both fin and scale you may eat ,      but all those that lack either fin or scale you shall not eat ; they be unclean for you .     you may eat all clean bird .       but you shall not eat any of the follow : the griffon vulture , the bearded vulture , the black vulture ,      the various kite and falcon ,      all kind of crow ,      the eagle owl , the kestrel , the long - eared owl , all specie of hawk ,      the little owl , the screech owl , the barn owl ,      the horned owl , the osprey , the cormorant ,      the stork , any kind of heron , the hoopoe , and the bat .       all winged insect be also unclean for you and shall not be eat .      any clean winged creature you may eat .     you shall not eat the carcass of any animal that have die of itself ; but you may give it to a resident alien within your gate to eat , or you may sell it to a foreigner . for you be a people holy to the   Lord , your God .   you shall not boil a young goat in its mother milk .     Tithes .     each year you shall tithe all the produce of your seed that grow in the field ;        then in the place which the   Lord , your God , choose as the dwelling place of his name    you shall eat in his presence the tithe of your grain , wine and oil , as well as the firstling of your herd and flock , that you may learn always to fear the   Lord , your God .       but if , when the   Lord , your God , bless you , the journey be too much for you and you be not able to bring your tithe , because the place which the   Lord , your God , choose to put his name be too far for you ,      you may exchange the tithe for money , and with the money securely in hand , go to the place which the   Lord , your God , choose .      you may then exchange the money for whatever you desire , oxen or sheep , wine or beer , or anything else you want , and there in the presence of the   Lord , your God , you shall consume it and rejoice , you and your household together .        but do not neglect the Levite within your gate , for he have no hereditary portion with you .       at the end of every third year you shall bring out all the tithe of your produce for that year and deposit they within your own community ,      that the Levite who have no hereditary portion with you , and also the resident alien , the orphan and the widow within your gate , may come and eat and be satisfied ; so that the   Lord , your God , may bless you in all that you undertake .
