b '   Feast of the Passover .     observe the month of Abib    by keep the Passover of the   Lord , your God ,    since it be in the month of Abib that the   Lord , your God , bring you out of Egypt by night .      you shall offer the Passover sacrifice from your flock and your herd to the   Lord , your God , in the place the   Lord   will choose as the dwelling place of his name .         you shall not eat leaven bread with it . for seven day you shall eat with it only unleavene bread , the bread of affliction , so that you may remember as long as you live the day you leave the land of Egypt ; for in hurried flight you leave the land of Egypt .      no leaven be to be find with you in all your territory for seven day , and none of the meat which you sacrifice on the evening of the first day shall be keep overnight for the next day .     you may not sacrifice the Passover in any of the community which the   Lord , your God , give you ;      only at the place which the   Lord , your God , will choose as the dwelling place of his name , and in the evening at sunset , at the very time when you leave Egypt , shall you sacrifice the Passover .        you shall cook and eat it at the place the   Lord , your God , will choose ; then in the morning you may return to your tent .      for six day you shall eat unleavened bread , and on the seventh day there shall be a solemn assembly for the   Lord , your God ; on that day you shall do no work .     Feast of Weeks .      you shall count off seven week ; begin to count the seven week from the day when the sickle be first put to the stand grain .      you shall then keep the feast of week    for the   Lord , your God , and the measure of your own voluntary offering which you will give shall be in proportion to the blessing the   Lord , your God , have give you .      you shall rejoice in the presence of the   Lord , your God , together with your son and daughter , your male and female slave , and the Levite within your gate , as well as the resident alien , the orphan , and the widow among you , in the place which the   Lord , your God , will choose as the dwelling place of his name .        remember that you too be slave in Egypt , so carry out these statute carefully .    Feast of booth .      you shall celebrate the feast of booth    for seven day , when you have gather in the produce from your threshing floor and wine press .      you shall rejoice at your feast ,    together with your son and daughter , your male and female slave , and also the Levite , the resident alien , the orphan and the widow within your gate .      for seven day you shall celebrate this feast for the   Lord , your God , in the place which the   Lord   will choose ; since the   Lord , your God , have bless you in all your crop and in all your undertaking , you will be full of joy .     three time a year ,    then , all your male shall appear before the   Lord , your God , in the place which he will choose : at the feast of Unleavened Bread , at the feast of Weeks , and at the feast of booth . they shall not appear before the   Lord   empty - handed ,      but each with his own gift , in proportion to the blessing which the   Lord , your God , have give to you .    Justice .      in all the community which the   Lord , your God , be give you , you shall appoint judge and official throughout your tribe to administer true justice for the people .      you must not distort justice : you shall not show partiality ;    you shall not take a bribe , for a bribe blind the eye even of the wise and twist the word even of the just .      justice , justice alone shall you pursue , so that you may live and possess the land the   Lord , your God , be give you .    Illicit Worship .      you shall not plant an asherah    of any kind of wood next to the altar of the   Lord , your God , which you will build ;        nor shall you erect a sacred pillar , such as the   Lord , your God , hate .
