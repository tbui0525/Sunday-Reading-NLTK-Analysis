I be come quickly . hold fast to what you have , so that no one may take your crown .      the victor I will make into a pillar    in the temple of my God , and he will never leave it again . on he I will inscribe the name of my God and the name of the city of my God , the new Jerusalem , which come down out of heaven from my God , as well as my new name .      whoever have ear ought to hear what the Spirit say to the church . to Laodicea .        to the angel of the church in Laodicea ,    write this : the amen , the faithful and true witness , the source of Gods creation , say this :        I know your work ; I know that you be neither cold nor hot .    I wish you be either cold or hot .       so , because you be lukewarm , neither hot nor cold , I will spit you out of my mouth .        for you say , I be rich and affluent and have no need of anything , and yet do not realize that you be wretched , pitiable , poor , blind , and naked .      I advise you to buy from I gold refine by fire    so that you may be rich , and white garment to put on so that your shameful nakedness may not be expose , and buy ointment to smear on your eye so that you may see .      those whom I love , I reprove and chastise . be earnest , therefore , and repent .      behold , I stand at the door and knock . if anyone hear my voice and open the door , [ then ] I will enter his house and dine with he , and he with I .        I will give the victor the right to sit with I on my throne , as I myself first win the victory and sit with my Father on his throne .      whoever have ear ought to hear what the Spirit say to the church .
