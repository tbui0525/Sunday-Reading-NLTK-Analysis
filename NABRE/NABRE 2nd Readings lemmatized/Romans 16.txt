I commend to you Phoebe our sister , who be [ also ] a minister    of the church at Cenchreae ,        that you may receive she in the Lord in a manner worthy of the holy one , and help she in whatever she may need from you , for she have be a benefactor to many and to I as well .    Pauls Greetings .     Greet Prisca and Aquila ,    my co - worker in Christ Jesus ,        who risk their neck for my life , to whom not only I be grateful but also all the church of the Gentiles ;      greet also the church at their house .    Greet my beloved Epaenetus , who be the firstfruit in Asia for Christ .        Greet Mary , who have work hard for you .      Greet Andronicus and Junia ,    my relative and my fellow prisoner ; they be prominent among the apostle and they be in Christ before I .      Greet Ampliatus , my beloved in the Lord .      Greet Urbanus , our co - worker in Christ , and my beloved Stachys .      Greet Apelles , who be approve in Christ . greet those who belong to the family of Aristobulus .      Greet my relative Herodion . greet those in the Lord who belong to the family of Narcissus .      Greet those worker in the Lord , Tryphaena and Tryphosa . Greet the beloved Persis , who have work hard in the Lord .      Greet Rufus ,    choose in the Lord , and his mother and mine .        Greet Asyncritus , Phlegon , Hermes , Patrobas , Hermas , and the brother who be with they .      Greet Philologus , Julia , Nereus and his sister , and Olympas , and all the holy one who be with they .      Greet one another with a holy kiss . all the church of Christ greet you .     against faction .      I urge you , brother , to watch out for those who create dissension and obstacle , in opposition to the teaching that you learn ; avoid they .        for such people do not serve our Lord Christ but their own appetite , and by fair and flattering speech they deceive the heart of the innocent .        for while your obedience be know to all , so that I rejoice over you , I want you to be wise as to what be good , and simple as to what be evil ;        then the God of peace will quickly crush Satan    under your foot . the grace of our Lord Jesus be with you .     greeting from Corinth .
