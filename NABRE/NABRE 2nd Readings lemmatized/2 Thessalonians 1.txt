b '    Greeting .      Paul , Silvanus , and Timothy to the church of the Thessalonians in God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ :        grace to you and peace from God [ our ] Father and the Lord Jesus Christ .    Thanksgiving .      we ought to thank God always for you , brother , as be fitting , because your faith flourish ever more , and the love of every one of you for one another grow ever great .        accordingly , we ourselves boast of you in the church of God regard your endurance and faith in all your persecution and the affliction you endure .     this be evidence of the just judgment of God , so that you may be consider worthy of the kingdom of God for which you be suffer .        for it be surely just on Gods part to repay with affliction those who be afflict you ,      and to grant rest along with we to you who be undergo affliction , at the revelation of the Lord Jesus from heaven with his mighty angel ,      in blaze fire , inflict punishment on those who do not acknowledge God and on those who do not obey the gospel of our Lord Jesus .        these will pay the penalty of eternal ruin , separate from the presence of the Lord and from the glory of his power ,        when he come to be glorify among his holy one    and to be marvel at on that day among all who have believe , for our testimony to you be believe .     Prayer .     to this end , we always pray for you , that our God may make you worthy of his calling and powerfully bring to fulfillment every good purpose and every effort of faith ,         that the name of our Lord Jesus may be glorify in you , and you in he ,    in accord with the grace of our God and Lord Jesus Christ .
