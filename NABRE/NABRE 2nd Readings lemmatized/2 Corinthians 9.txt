b '   Gods Indescribable Gift .      now about the service to the holy one , it be superfluous for I to write to you ,      for I know your eagerness , about which I boast of you to the Macedonians , that Achaia    have be ready since last year ; and your zeal have stir up most of they .        nonetheless , I send the brother    so that our boast about you might not prove empty in this case , so that you might be ready , as I say ,      for fear that if any Macedonians come with I and find you not ready we might be put to shame ( to say nothing of you ) in this conviction .      so I think it necessary to encourage the brother to go on ahead to you and arrange in advance for your promise gift , so that in this way it might be ready as a bountiful gift and not as an exaction .     consider this : whoever sow sparingly will also reap sparingly , and whoever sow bountifully will also reap bountifully .        each must do as already determine , without sadness or compulsion , for God love a cheerful giver .         moreover , God be able to make every grace abundant for you , so that in all thing , always have all you need , you may have an abundance for every good work .      as it be write : he scatter abroad , he give to the poor ;   his righteousness endure forever .      the one who supply seed to the sower and bread for food will supply and multiply your seed and increase the harvest of your righteousness .       you be be enrich in every way for all generosity , which through we produce thanksgive to God ,      for the administration of this public service be not only supply the need of the holy one but be also overflow in many act of thanksgiving to God .      through the evidence of this service , you be glorify God for your obedient confession of the gospel of Christ and the generosity of your contribution to they and to all other ,        while in prayer on your behalf they long for you , because of the surpass grace of God upon you .      thank be to God for his indescribable gift !
