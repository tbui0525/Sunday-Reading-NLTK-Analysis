b' Prayer of a Returned Exile  A song of ascents.  The  Lord  answered me  when I called in my distress:   Lord , deliver my soul from lying lips,  from a treacherous tongue.     What will he inflict on you,  O treacherous tongue,  and what more besides?   A warriors arrows  sharpened with coals of brush wood!       Alas, I am a foreigner in Meshech,  I live among the tents of Kedar!  Too long do I live  among those who hate peace.  When I speak of peace,  they are for war.             '
