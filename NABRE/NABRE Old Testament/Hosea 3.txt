b'  Hosea and His Wife Reunited     Again the  Lord  said to me: Go, love a woman  who is loved by her spouse but commits adultery; Just as the  Lord  loves the Israelites,  though they turn to other gods  and love raisin cakes.      So I acquired her for myself for fifteen pieces of silver and a homer and a lethech of barley.     Then I said to her: You will wait for me for many days;  you will not prostitute yourself Or belong to any man;  I in turn will wait for you.   For the Israelites will remain many days  without king or prince, Without sacrifice or sacred pillar,  without ephod or household gods.  Afterward the Israelites will turn back  and seek the  Lord , their God,  and David, their king;   They will come trembling to the  Lord  and to his bounty, in the last days.            '
