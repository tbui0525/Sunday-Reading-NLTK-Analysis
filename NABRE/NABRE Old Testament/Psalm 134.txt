b' Exhortation to the Night Watch to Bless God  A song of ascents. O come, bless the  Lord ,  all you servants of the  Lord  You who stand in the house of the  Lord  throughout the nights.   Lift up your hands toward the sanctuary,   and bless the  Lord .  May the  Lord  bless you from Zion,  the Maker of heaven and earth.             '
