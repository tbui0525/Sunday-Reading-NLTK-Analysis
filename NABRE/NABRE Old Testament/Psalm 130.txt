b' Prayer for Pardon and Mercy  A song of ascents. I Out of the depths   I call to you,  Lord ;   Lord, hear my cry! May your ears be attentive  to my cry for mercy.   If you,  Lord , keep account of sins,  Lord, who can stand?   But with you is forgiveness  and so you are revered.    II  I wait for the  Lord ,  my soul waits  and I hope for his word.   My soul looks for the Lord  more than sentinels for daybreak.  More than sentinels for daybreak,   let Israel hope in the  Lord , For with the  Lord  is mercy,  with him is plenteous redemption,   And he will redeem Israel  from all its sins.             '
