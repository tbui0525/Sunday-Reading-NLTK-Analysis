b' Zion the True Birthplace   A psalm of the Korahites. A song. I  His foundation is on holy mountains,  The  Lord  loves the gates   of Zion  more than any dwelling in Jacob.  Glorious things are said of you,  O city of God! Selah   II  Rahab and Babylon I count  among those who know me. See, Philistia and Tyre, with Ethiopia,  This one was born there.   And of Zion it will be said:  Each one was born in it.  The Most High will establish it;    the  Lord  notes in the register of the peoples:  This one was born there.  Selah  So singers and dancers:  All my springs are in you.             '
