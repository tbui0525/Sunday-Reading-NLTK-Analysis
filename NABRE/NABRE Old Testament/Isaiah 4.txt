Jerusalem Purified    On that day, The branch   of the  Lord  will be beauty and glory,  and the fruit of the land will be honor and splendor  for the survivors of Israel.  Everyone who remains in Zion,  everyone left in Jerusalem Will be called holy:  everyone inscribed for life   in Jerusalem.   When the Lord washes away  the filth of the daughters of Zion, And purges Jerusalems blood from her midst  with a blast of judgment, a searing blast,   Then will the  Lord  create,  over the whole site of Mount Zion  and over her place of assembly, A smoking cloud by day  and a light of flaming fire by night.   For over all, his glory will be shelter and protection:  shade from the parching heat of day,  refuge and cover from storm and rain.             '
