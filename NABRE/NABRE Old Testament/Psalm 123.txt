b' Reliance on the Lord  A song of ascents. To you I raise my eyes,  to you enthroned in heaven.   Yes, like the eyes of servants  on the hand of their masters, Like the eyes of a maid  on the hand of her mistress, So our eyes are on the  Lord  our God,  till we are shown favor.  Show us favor,  Lord , show us favor,  for we have our fill of contempt.   Our souls are more than sated  with mockery from the insolent,  with contempt from the arrogant.            '
