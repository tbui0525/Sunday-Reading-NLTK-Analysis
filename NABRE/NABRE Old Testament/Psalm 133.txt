b' A Vision of a Blessed Community  A song of ascents. Of David. How good and how pleasant it is,  when brothers   dwell together as one!  Like fine oil on the head,    running down upon the beard, Upon the beard of Aaron,  upon the collar of his robe.  Like dew   of Hermon coming down  upon the mountains of Zion.  There the  Lord  has decreed a blessing,  life for evermore!             '
