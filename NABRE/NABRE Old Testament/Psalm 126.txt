b' The Reversal of Zions Fortunes  A song of ascents. I When the  Lord  restored the captives of Zion,   we thought we were dreaming.  Then our mouths were filled with laughter;  our tongues sang for joy.  Then it was said among the nations,  The  Lord  had done great things for them.  The  Lord  has done great things for us;  Oh, how happy we were!  Restore our captives,  Lord ,  like the dry stream beds of the Negeb.    II  Those who sow in tears  will reap with cries of joy.   Those who go forth weeping,  carrying sacks of seed, Will return with cries of joy,  carrying their bundled sheaves.            '
