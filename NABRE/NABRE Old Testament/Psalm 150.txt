b' Final Doxology  Hallelujah! Praise God in his holy sanctuary;    give praise in the mighty dome of heaven.  Give praise for his mighty deeds,   praise him for his great majesty.  Give praise with blasts upon the horn,   praise him with harp and lyre.  Give praise with tambourines and dance,  praise him with strings and pipes.   Give praise with crashing cymbals,  praise him with sounding cymbals.  Let everything that has breath  give praise to the  Lord !  Hallelujah!            '
